# CareerLens AI

## Overview

CareerLens AI is an AI-powered career intelligence platform that helps job seekers track applications, analyze hiring outcomes, identify skill gaps, and receive personalized career recommendations.

## Problem Statement

Job seekers often apply to hundreds of jobs but do not understand:
- Why they are getting rejected
- Which roles suit them best
- Which skills are missing
- How to improve their chances

CareerLens AI converts job-search activity into actionable insights using AI and analytics.
