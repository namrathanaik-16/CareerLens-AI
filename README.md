

# CareerLens AI

An AI-powered career intelligence platform that helps job seekers track applications, analyze hiring outcomes, identify skill gaps, and receive personalized career recommendations.

---

## Problem Statement

Job seekers often apply to hundreds of jobs but struggle to understand:

* Why they are getting rejected
* Which roles fit their profile best
* Which skills they are missing
* How to improve their chances of getting hired

CareerLens AI transforms job-search data into actionable insights using artificial intelligence and analytics.
